readout lab

Aesthetic tests for the Manhattan GPS Watch /live cross-street readout. Same content in every card — the street × avenue number and the neighborhood — so you can compare the type, color, and treatment head-to-head. Card 01 is a refined take on the current direction (with a bit more highlight); the rest range from understated to loud. Pick one (or mix) by its number/name.
